# LinkedIn Post Draft

I am working on a data analytics portfolio project titled **Albania's Brain Drain: A Data-Driven Analysis of Migration, Employment and Economic Development**.

The project studies how demographic and economic indicators are connected to migration pressure in Albania. It uses public datasets from the World Bank, Eurostat, and official reports to analyze population decline, migration flows, residence permits, unemployment, GDP growth, and demographic risk.

Tools and skills used:

- Python
- Pandas
- SQL / PostgreSQL
- Power BI
- Statistics
- Forecasting
- Data visualization
- Research reporting

The goal is to build a complete end-to-end analytics project, from raw public data to insights, dashboard, and final research report.
