# Exploratory Data Analysis Summary

## Project

Albania’s Brain Drain: A Data-Driven Analysis of Migration, Employment and Economic Development

## Purpose

This report summarizes the main descriptive patterns found during exploratory data analysis.

## Population

- Population in 2000: 3,089,027
- Latest available population year: 2024
- Latest available population: 2,377,128
- Population change since 2000: -711,899
- Population change percentage since 2000: -23.05%

## Net Migration

- Strongest negative net migration year in the dataset: 2000
- Net migration value in that year: -60,531

## Top Destination Countries

- Germany: 16,828,903
- Spain: 11,146,851
- France: 6,548,079
- United Kingdom: 6,299,026
- Italy: 5,578,003

## Residence Permits by Reason

Latest available permit year: 2025

- Family reasons: 35,478
- Education reasons: 11,102
- Employment reasons: 7,584

## Important Limitations

- Eurostat immigration data shows Albanian citizens arriving in European reporting countries, not direct emigration from Albania.
- Residence permit TOTAL categories were separated from reason subcategories to avoid double counting.
- Correlation analysis does not prove causation.
- Some indicators have missing values depending on the source and year.
