# Data Limitations

1. Eurostat migration-flow data does not directly measure all emigration from Albania. It records Albanian citizens moving into or out of European reporting countries.
2. Residence permits show legal permits issued, not all migration events.
3. Some World Bank indicators may have missing years.
4. Different data sources may use different definitions and update schedules.
5. Forecasts to 2035 are estimates, not certain predictions.
6. Brain drain is difficult to measure directly because education level and skill level are not always available in migration datasets.
